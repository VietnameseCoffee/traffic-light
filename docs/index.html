Traffic Light Simulator

Control a traffic light as cars zoom by during rush hour in New Townsville.

INSTRUCTIONS: Buttons appear on the bottom row below.

LIGHT: Start and Stop buttons to cycle on and off the traffic light.

LANES: Signals which lane the manually generated cars come from. Can be seen with a red indicator at the right end of the street.

CARS: Create on demand the three types of cars. Randomly generate cars by toggling the Auto button. Auto generate creates cars in any lane.

Some cars are faster than others like the sports car zooming by compared to the Bug, but it also depends on your browser too!

Each car uses a different JavaScript mechanism to move, specifically requestAnimationFrame, setTimeout, setInterval (respective to the buttons). This simulation showcases their different callback rates in the event loop, and different browser mechanism. Try this simulator out on different browsers and see variations in performance and speed!
